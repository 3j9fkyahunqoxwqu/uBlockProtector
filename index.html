Why use AdBlockers?

Ads are important for websites to sustain themselves. However, some websites use ads that are annoying or even force the user to click them. If you believe websites should be allowed to use any form of ads they want, then many websites use tracking methods to display targeted ads, and that can go wrong. The company might promise you to only use that information to choose ads, but why should you trust them? You should have the control over your privacy, and choose which website to trust.

Recently, many websites start to employ methods to detect and sometimes track AdBlockers and ask or sometimes even force the user to disable them even though trying to detect AdBlockers is illegal in some places. The good news is, there are ways to protect AdBlockers from being detected.

How to protect my AdBlockers?

1. Adblock Warning Removal List

2. Anti-Adblock Killer

3. This project: AdBlock Protector

Make sure to install Anti-Adblock Killer BEFORE AdBlock Protector for compatibility!

When a protection mechanism is implemented into one of the larger projects, it will be removed from this one. This project is meant to be a temporary solution while waiting for larger projects to update. You should keep all of these installed.

Why not create pull requests to existing projects?

It will take time before it gets added, and our goal is quick solutions. We will send our solutions to larger projects such as Anti-Adblock Killer.

Some of our codes can be unstable or inneficient, but that is the tradeoff for quick solutions.

How do I use it?

1. Install a script host such as Tampermonkey, you should already have it installed. I will try to offer support for all major browsers and script hosts, but Chrome and Opera with Tampermonkey for sure.

2. Install our Userscript.

3. See if your website requires special rules or scripts.

How do I report a bug or a website?

If you have a bug or a suggestion that you want to let me know, or have a website that is not supported to report, please create an issue here.

If you are reporting a website, please provide the link to the website in question, and a screenshot, also, you should report it to larger projects as well.
